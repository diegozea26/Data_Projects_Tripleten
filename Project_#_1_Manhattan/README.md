# Manhattan Vacation Rental Market Analysis

## Overview:
The goal of this analysis is to identify the most common neighborhoods and property sizes in the Manhattan vacation rental market, as well as to highlight top-performing listings to support investment decisions.

## Tools used: 
- Google Sheets

## Key Insights: 
- - The top 10 most attractive neighborhoods report a number of reviews ranging from over 2000 to more than 6,000.

- The most used places rented are those which come with one, two or three bedrooms.
